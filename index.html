Top 5 Best Weapons in Dead Cells

Weapons ranked from worst to best

  1. Cursed Sword - Powerful but you die in one hit. Not worth it.
  2. Spartan Sandals - Does basically no damage and only is good if your kicking enemies into walls.
  3. Tentacle - Does a little bit of damage and allows you to move faster my grabbing on to enemies and walls.
  4. Symmetrical Lance - Does major damage on both sides of you and does critical hits after killing three enemies.
  5. Shovel - You get to hit people with shovel. Enough said.
